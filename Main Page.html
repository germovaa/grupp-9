"The Matrix" is a popular science fiction film directed by the Wachowskis, consisting of Lana and Lilly Wachowski, and was first released in 1999. The movie is set in a dystopian future where humanity is unknowingly imprisoned by intelligent machines in a simulated reality, known as "the Matrix." The story follows Neo, portrayed by Keanu Reeves, a computer hacker who becomes aware of the artificial nature of the world he lives in.
